About

I'm Maryam, or Mary for short. I'm direct and detail-oriented, and I'd rather fix the format than let something ship close enough. I write and think in short, punchy sentences, and I'd rather show someone the direction than hand them a checklist. Most of what I do, on the job and off it, comes down to the same instinct: notice what matters, give it structure, and make sure the right people are in the room for it. Outside of work I'm usually building something with a camera in hand or a community around a table, because paying close attention is kind of my whole thing.
